The 5 Ps · per task

Prohibit · keep AI out. Sensitive, official, or the values call.
Polish · refine what you wrote. Never originate it.
Plan · think it through with you. You decide.
Produce · draft with you, on your standard.
Partner · co-author. You own and answer for it.

Postures, not a ladder. Set per task, by data and stakes. Partner does not beat Prohibit.
